    • Potato Biryani Recipe

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups basmati rice
    • 3 medium potatoes (peeled and cut into cubes)
    • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
    • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
    • 1 cup yogurt
    • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
    • 3 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee
    • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)
    • Salt to taste
    • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cardamom, cloves)
    • Water for cooking rice

    Steps

    1. Rinse the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
    2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee and add whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves).
    3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
    4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
    5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
    6. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, and biryani masala.
    7. Add potato cubes and cook for 5–7 minutes until slightly tender.
    8. Stir in yogurt, coriander, and mint leaves. Cook for another 2 minutes.
    9. In a separate pot, boil the soaked rice until it is 70% cooked, then drain the water.
    10. Layer the rice over the potato mixture. Sprinkle some more mint and coriander leaves on top.
    11. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 15–20 minutes.
    12. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 5 minutes before gently mixing.
    13. Serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy your delicious potato biryani!

    Tip: You can also add fried onions or a few saffron strands soaked in milk for extra flavor and aroma.
